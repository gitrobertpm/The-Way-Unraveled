MISSION STATEMENT

In the beginning, this site will serve as little more than my own personal code laboratory and portfolio.

Besides style, design, and user-interface, it will have a focus on compassion, inspiration, and the betterment of humankind.

The hope is that after I tinker around a bit, and get a few things beyond the idea stage, just maybe this site will be able to do some good for those who need it.

And the goal is to contribute something to the great and noble movement that is reaching to make the world a safer, more enjoyable, and easier place for all to live.

Come on in. Have a look around.
